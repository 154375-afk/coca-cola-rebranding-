Creating a stronger and more memorable identity for Coca-Cola Zero

What is Coca-Cola Zero?

Coca-Cola Zero Sugar is a sugar-free soft drink created by Coca-Cola to provide consumers with the classic Coca-Cola taste without the sugar and calories. The product was designed for people who want a healthier alternative while still enjoying the familiar flavour of Coca-Cola. Since its launch, Coca-Cola Zero has become one of the company's most successful products and is now sold in countries all around the world. Because the product is widely recognised, its branding and visual identity play an important role in attracting customers and influencing purchasing decisions.

Problems with the Current Design

Although the current Coca-Cola Zero design is recognisable, research identified several weaknesses within the branding. The design relies heavily on the existing Coca-Cola identity, making it difficult for Coca-Cola Zero to develop its own unique appearance. The packaging uses only a limited number of visual elements and therefore may not stand out strongly when displayed alongside competing products. In addition, modern packaging trends often use strong visual hierarchy and memorable symbols, which are not fully present within the current design. This may reduce the product's ability to attract younger audiences and create a lasting impression.

Limited visual uniqueness
Very similar to other Coca-Cola products
Weak shelf impact compared to competitors
Minimal distinctive branding elements
Less appealing to younger consumers

Why Rebranding is Needed

Consumer preferences and design trends constantly change over time. In order to remain competitive, companies must regularly update their visual identity to meet the expectations of their target audience. Rebranding Coca-Cola Zero provides an opportunity to create a stronger and more memorable identity while maintaining the trust and recognition associated with the Coca-Cola name. A refreshed design can improve shelf visibility, attract attention, and help the product stand out from competitors. It can also communicate a clearer message and create a stronger connection with modern consumers.

Current Logo vs New Logo

The current Coca-Cola Zero branding successfully uses the famous Coca-Cola script, which provides strong global recognition. However, it lacks a unique symbol that clearly separates Coca-Cola Zero from other products within the Coca-Cola range. My redesigned logo introduces a crown symbol while keeping the classic Coca-Cola typography. This approach allows the design to remain familiar to existing customers while creating a more distinctive identity. The new logo aims to improve recognition and make the product appear more premium and memorable.

How the New Logo Improves the Brand

The crown symbol was chosen because it represents quality, leadership and premium value. By introducing this new visual element, the logo becomes more distinctive and easier to recognise. The crown creates a clear focal point that immediately attracts attention while still preserving the traditional Coca-Cola script. This balance between familiarity and innovation ensures that existing customers will still recognise the brand while younger consumers may find the updated appearance more modern and appealing. Overall, the new logo creates a stronger visual identity that supports the goals of the rebranding project.
